Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate Chip cookies are a delicious dessert for everyone.

Ingredients

  • Cup of Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • 3 Cups Flour
  • Half Stick Butter
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 2 Eggs

Directions

  1. First, put the flour, butter, sugar and eggs into a large mixing bowl.
  2. Use a whisk to mix the items until uniform consistency.
  3. After mixing, put small cirles of the dough onto a cookie sheet. They should be about three inches in diameter.
  4. Put chocolate chips into each cookie.
  5. Put the cookie sheets into the oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
